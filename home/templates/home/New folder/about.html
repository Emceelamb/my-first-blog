About

I'm an artist of all kinds of mediums primarily in the digital realm. Currently in the Bay Area.

Artist Statement


My practice seeks to disrupts the notion that everything has a place and everything in in its place. I explore the meaning of art making and the boundaries of artwork using analog and digital technologies not typically associated with fine art. At the core of my work, I am concerned with the nature of identity. Drawing on media studies and critical theory, my practice seeks to redefine given identities and explore the reification of objects.

Using technology as a medium, I try to understand its impact on the rapidly evolving human experience. The digital realm has been tightly embedded in our culture and I attempt to reveal how this ever present digital surveillance and the diffusions of innovations impacts the world.

You can describe my aesthetic as experimental. As a contrarian at heart, I reject the easy solutions to a design problem. Instead, I push myself to create something different or with a new perspective. You can contact me for more information.
